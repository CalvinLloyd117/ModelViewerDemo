Augmented Reality

Augmented Reality is on the rise!

What is Augmented Reality?

In general, Augmented Reality is an interactive experience in which computer generated visuals are placed into real-world enviornments.

Augmented Reality in Model-Viewer


Model-Viewer supports AR by default, allowing users to place our 3D models into their environments at home or school, and interact with them in a unique way using Google's Scene Viewer.

All that is required for AR is that the ar tag be included in the Model-Viewer html code. Then, all supported devices have the ability to activate AR.

Unfortunately, I cannot seem to get the AR working for our models. However, the working example at https://modelviewer.dev/examples/augmented-reality.html proves that AR in Model-Viewer works.
