Augmented Reality

Augmented Reality is on the Rise!

What is Augmented Reality?

In general, Augmented Reality is an interactive experience in which computer generated visuals are placed into real-world enviornments.

Augmented Reality in Model-Viewer

Model-Viewer supports AR by default, allowing users to place our 3D models into their enviornments at home or School, and interact with them in a unique way using Google's Scene Viewer.

This feature is supported by default, and all that is required is that the ar tag be included in the Model-Viewer html code.
