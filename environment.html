Environment and Lighting

Transparent Backgrounds

Backgrounds in Model-Viewer can be set to be transparent, allowing an image to be seen behind the model.

NOTE: This feature is only present in an unreleased development version of Model-Viewer.

We are able to set the background of a model to a .gif aswell.

An Equirectangular HDR skybox-image

HDR skybox images provide a 3D enviornment for the model to live in.

Ex. skybox-image="images\whipple_creek_regional_park_04_1k.hdr"



Additionally, HDR (and LDR) Skyboxes supply lighting to certain models where applicable.

HDR Skyboxes vs. LDR Skyboxes

Model-Viewer allows for two Types of Skyboxes, namely HDR and LDR Skyboxes. HDR Skyboxes provide brighter, more accurate lighting effects and reflections than LDR.





Environment Images

Environment-images in Model-viewer provide the lighting and reflection effects of a Skybox, without the 3D environment around the object.



HDR Skyboxes vs. Environment Images

Environment Images are defined independantly of the skybox, and background. They are also not exclusive. One Environment image may be used with a totally different Skybox.

Environment Image Options




HDR Skybox Options




If Environment Images are disabled/not defined, then the model will take on the lighting and reflection effects of the Skybox. However, if the instance of the model has both environment-image and skybox-image defined, then the model will take on the lighting and reflections of the environment-image, but with the 3D skybox defined by the skybox-image attribute.
